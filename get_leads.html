Get quality leads Everyday!

Businesses are always in the hunt for new leads but it’s always difficult to come by. As a business owner, you are always looking to try out and implement various strategies that would yield you new quality leads.

With Spot Offers you get various options to receive new quality leads. Users when viewing your offers have the option to request a call back if they need more information on your offer or need a better price. This lead is delivered straight to your merchant app. You can then call the customer directly to convert them to a sale.

Another method by which you can receive leads is via user price request. We allow users to submit a price request form. This request is then delivered to all the merchants in the relevant category and location. You can view these requests in our merchant app and reply to their request. This option allows you to connect to new customers who would otherwise, not visit your store.
